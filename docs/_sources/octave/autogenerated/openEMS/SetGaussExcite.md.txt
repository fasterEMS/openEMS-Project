SetGaussExcite
==============
```{function}  SetGaussExcite(FDTD,f0,fc);
```
Full definition:
```{code-block} matlab
function FDTD = SetGaussExcite(FDTD,f0,fc);
```

- f0 : center frequency
- fc : 20dB cutoff frequency --> bandwidth is 2*fc

see also SetSinusExcite SetCustomExcite

e.g

    FDTD = SetGaussExcite(FDTD,1e9,1e8);
